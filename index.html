    Sights On The Heights #

    What? #

    Sights On The Heights is a phrase that I coined during the 2022 Ottawa Army run

    Why? #

    Apart from figuratively meaning this, I was looking up at the tall skyscrappers around downtown Ottawa and realized that instead of jumping of one, I wanted to be inside one. To put it another way, I set my sights on the heights.

    Who #

    I’m a 4th year computer science student looking to use technology to improve the world. Vague words and huge task, but I hope this blog can inspire others to dream big and work their way up.
